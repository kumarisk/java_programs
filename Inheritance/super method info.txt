super method
1) it is a method and used to call the constrcutor of super class from the sub class
2) super method is always used inside the sub class to invoke the parametrised constructor of super class explicitly
3) we cant use super method inside the super class
4) super method is always used in Inheritance relationship
5) super method cant used inside the method of sub class, it is always  used inside the cosntructor of sub class
6) super method should be first statement inside the constructor of susb class
